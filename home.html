How HumSafar Works

Our platform offers intuitive features designed for both elderly parents and their children, making staying connected simple and meaningful.

Video Calls

Schedule and join video calls with a simple interface designed for all ages. Stay face-to-face even when miles apart.

Health Tracking

Monitor medication schedules, doctor appointments, and daily health metrics to ensure wellbeing.

Mood Check-ins

Track emotional wellbeing with simple emoji-based mood logging, helping families stay attuned to each other's emotional needs.

Grocery Ordering

Children can order groceries and essentials for their parents, with easy tracking and delivery updates.

Messaging

Simple, intuitive messaging with support for text, emojis, and media sharing to stay connected throughout the day.

SOS Alerts

Emergency button for parents that instantly notifies children when help is needed, providing peace of mind.

Intuitive Dashboards

Designed with both generations in mind, our dashboards are easy to navigate and provide all the essential features at your fingertips.
